RELEFORD IS LIVE

The Only Artist To Showcase On Mars

As a young boy growing up in West Africa, Ryan was inspired by the colors and the natural beauty of the tropical grasslands that make up the African savanna. Rainfall was infrequent during the long dry season, but when it came, the clouds and storms that accompanied it inspired Ryan's earliest paintings. Throughout his school years, Ryan excelled at art and gained recognition by his teachers. In fact, his high school art teacher was so impressed with Ryan's work that he encouraged him to advance his studies in art after high school, but Ryan decided to pursue a more financially stable path. At the age of 21, Ryan moved to London, where he lived for 7 years, pursuing many different career paths and entrepreneurial ventures while always dreaming of going back to painting full-time. Ryan currently lives in Los Angeles.
